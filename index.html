Joji

Musician | Artist | Inspiration

About Joji

Joji (George Kusunoki Miller), born George Miller, is a Japanese-Australian musician, singer, songwriter, record producer, and former internet personality. His artistic journey began as a YouTube personality creating comedic and often surreal content under the name Filthy Frank. Transitioning to the music scene, Joji has captivated audiences with his unique blend of R&B, lo-fi, and electronic elements.


Music Career

Joji's music career took off with the release of his debut EP, "In Tongues," in 2017. He followed up with a string of successful singles and albums, including "Ballads 1" (2018) and "Nectar" (2020). Joji's soulful voice, emotionally charged lyrics, and experimental soundscapes have earned him critical acclaim and a dedicated fanbase.

Why Joji Inspires

Joji's ability to transcend genres and convey raw emotions through his music is truly inspiring. The way he weaves emotions into his tunes, it's like he's putting words and melodies to feelings I can't express. His authenticity, vulnerability, and willingness to explore different artistic mediums demonstrate a fearless approach to creativity. His creativity is just so inspiring. Joji's journey from internet content creator to a respected artist showcases the power of personal reinvention and pursuing one's true passion.
